Blueberry-Mascarpone French Toast

time 75min

serves 10

level intermediate

see ingredients below

  • 4 cups cubed French bread
  • 2 cups frozen blueberries
  • 2 cartons mascarpone cheese
  • ½ cup confectioners sugar
  • 10 slices French bread
  • 8 large eggs
  • 2 cups half-and-half cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • ⅓ cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup sliced almonds, toasted
see steps below
  • In a greased 13x9-in. baking dish, layer bread cubes and blueberries. In a small bowl, beat mascarpone cheese and confectioners' sugar until smooth; drop by tablespoonfuls over blueberries. Top with bread slices.
  • In a large bowl, whisk eggs, cream, milk, granulated sugar and vanilla; pour over bread. Refrigerate, covered, overnight.
  • Preheat oven to 350Â°. Remove French toast from refrigerator while oven heats. Bake, covered, 30 minutes. Bake, uncovered, 30-40 minutes longer or until puffed and golden and a knife inserted in the center comes out clean.
  • Let stand 10 minutes before serving. Dust with additional confectioners' sugar; sprinkle with almonds. If desired, serve with additional blueberries.
  • * Feel free to use provided timer below *
